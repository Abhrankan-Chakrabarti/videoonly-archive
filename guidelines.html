Submission Guidelines

VIDEOONLY exists to provide a quiet space for anonymous reflection.

To protect contributors, readers, and the project itself, all submissions must follow these guidelines.

What Is Allowed

  • Personal reflections and confessions
  • Experiences shared without identifying details
  • Thoughts expressed without targeting others
  • Content that is descriptive rather than accusatory

What Is Not Allowed

  • Real names, usernames, addresses, or locations
  • Identifying details about private individuals
  • Threats, harassment, or calls for harm
  • Confessions involving ongoing criminal activity
  • Instructions for illegal or harmful actions
  • Content intended to defame or expose others

Moderation Policy

All submissions are reviewed manually.

Submissions may be edited for clarity, length, or safety.

Submissions that do not meet these guidelines are discarded.

There is no appeal process and no correspondence regarding rejected submissions.

Privacy

VIDEOONLY does not collect analytics, identifiers, or metadata.

Published confessions are presented without timestamps, identifiers, or ordering that could be used to infer authorship.

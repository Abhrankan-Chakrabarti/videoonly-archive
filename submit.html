Submit a Confession

VIDEOONLY accepts anonymous confessions submitted over the Tor network.

Submissions are reviewed manually. There is no guarantee of publication.

How to Submit

Prepare your confession as plain text.

  • Do not include real names
  • Do not include locations or identifying details
  • Do not include threats or calls for harm
  • Do not include illegal instructions or admissions

Send your confession using one of the following methods:

  • PGP-encrypted email (preferred)
  • Tor-only anonymous mailbox (if provided)

Submission addresses are intentionally not listed publicly.

What Happens Next

All submissions are read privately.

Approved confessions may be edited for clarity or safety before publication.

Rejected submissions are discarded and not stored.

No metadata, IP addresses, or identifiers are collected.

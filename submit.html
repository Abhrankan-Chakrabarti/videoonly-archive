Submit a Confession

VIDEOONLY accepts anonymous confessions submitted over the Tor network.

Submissions are reviewed manually. There is no guarantee of publication.

How to Submit

Prepare your confession as plain text.

  • Do not include real names, usernames, or locations
  • Do not include identifying details about others
  • Do not include threats or calls for harm
  • Do not include instructions or admissions of illegal activity

Where to Send

Send your confession via Tor to:

videoonly.archive@protonmail.com

PGP encryption is recommended but not required.

If your submission is accepted, it may be lightly edited for clarity or safety before publication.

What Happens Next

All submissions are read privately.

Rejected submissions are discarded and not stored.

VIDEOONLY does not collect analytics, metadata, or identifying information.

VIDEOONLY

VIDEOONLY is a Tor-based anonymous confession archive.

Submissions are private.
Publication is selective.
Not all confessions are published.

This site is read-only. There are no comments, reactions, or accounts.

This project exists to give people a safe place to share things they cannot say elsewhere.

Confessions are not submitted through this website. They are sent privately using end-to-end encrypted email (PGP).

Only encrypted submissions are accepted. Unencrypted messages may be ignored.

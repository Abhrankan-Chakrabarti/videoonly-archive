Confession 001

I often feel like I am performing a version of myself that others expect, rather than expressing what I actually think or want.

This performance is not forced by anyone directly. It grew slowly, shaped by habits, routines, and the desire to avoid friction.

I do not think this makes me dishonest. But it does make me tired.

Writing this anonymously feels quieter than saying it out loud.
